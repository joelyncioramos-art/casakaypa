Small uppercase pill for amenities, seasons and categories; keep labels to one or two words.

```jsx
<Tag tone="sage">Wi-Fi</Tag>
<Tag tone="terracotta">Fogata</Tag>
<Tag tone="outline" size="sm">Verano</Tag>
```

Tones: `sage | seafoam | terracotta | rose | neutral | outline`. The pill radius is intentional here — one of the few components allowed to be fully rounded.
